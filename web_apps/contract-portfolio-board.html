Contract Portfolio Board

Use this board to turn a rough project or contract idea into a practical portfolio shape: add candidate artefacts, sort them into workstreams, move workstreams between contract packages, and keep context, concept-of-operations, or internal ownership items separate from supplier scope.

It is an interactive app of all main scope items, to allow you to allocate to a Contract, or to deal with internally.

  • Select an item then tap a work-stream to move it.
